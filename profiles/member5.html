About Viper

Viper is an initiator specialist with exceptional game sense and utility timing. Known for perfectly coordinated pushes and site executes, Viper helps teams break through the toughest defenses with well-timed flashes, recon, and utility usage.

As a Twitch partner, Viper streams regularly and interacts with the community, sharing insights on agent selection, team composition, and how to maximize your impact as an initiator. Whether playing Sova, Fade, or Breach, Viper demonstrates high-level initiator play.
