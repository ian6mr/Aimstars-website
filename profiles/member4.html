About Phoenix

Phoenix is a controller specialist who excels at map control and strategic smoke placements. With deep knowledge of Valorant's tactical gameplay, Phoenix creates content that helps players understand the importance of utility usage and team coordination.

Creating in-depth strategy videos and VOD reviews, Phoenix breaks down professional gameplay and teaches viewers how to apply high-level concepts to their own matches. Whether you're learning Omen, Brimstone, or Viper, Phoenix's guides will elevate your controller gameplay.
