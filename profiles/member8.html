About Reyna

Reyna is the ultimate frag hunter, streaming the ranked grind daily and showing viewers what it takes to climb the competitive ladder. With insane flick shots and an aggressive playstyle, Reyna demonstrates how to carry games through pure mechanical skill and confidence.

Streaming ranked matches from start to finish, Reyna provides real-time commentary on decision-making, positioning, and how to maintain a winning mindset even in difficult matches. Perfect for players who want to improve their aim and learn how to take control of games.
