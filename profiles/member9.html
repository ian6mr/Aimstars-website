About Omen

Omen is the comedian of Aimstars, creating hilarious content that reminds everyone that gaming is supposed to be fun. Specializing in funny moments compilations and highlight reels that showcase the lighter side of Valorant, Omen brings laughs and entertainment to the team's social media.

From perfectly timed jokes to unexpected clutches turned comedy gold, Omen's content captures the memorable moments that happen when you're just having a good time with friends. Perfect for when you need a break from serious ranked grind and just want to laugh.
