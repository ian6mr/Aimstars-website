About Cero

Cero is the energy of Aimstars - a duelist main who brings explosive entry fragging and high-octane entertainment to every stream. Known for viral TikTok clips and insane highlight plays, Cero has built a massive following with charismatic personality and aggressive playstyle.

Whether it's pulling off impossible aces, creating hilarious moments, or hyping up the community, Cero represents everything fun about Valorant. With millions of views across social media platforms, Cero proves that gaming content can be both entertaining and skillful.
