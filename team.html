Interested in becoming part of Aimstars?

Invite Only

Aimstars is an exclusive community. We carefully select vibrant content creators looking to join a supportive community. We're always on the lookout for talented individuals who share our passion for the game and creating engaging content for our audience.

Requirements

We look for creators who are active, passionate about Valorant, and dedicated to growing their audience. It doesn't matter as much as personality, commitment, and the ability to entertain and educate your audience consistently.

Business Inquiries

Ready to join the team? Reach out to us on Discord or send us an email with your content portfolio, streaming schedule, and why you'd be a great fit for Aimstars. We review all applications carefully.
