Community Guidelines

What to expect when you join the Aimstars community

Respectful Environment

We maintain a positive, welcoming space for all skill levels and backgrounds. Toxicity, harassment, and discrimination have no place here. Be kind, be supportive, and help make our community the best it can be.

Active Engagement

Our creators prioritize community interaction. Expect responses in Discord, engagement during streams, and genuine connection. We're here because we love this game and this community as much as you do.

Fast Response

Discord messages typically get responses within hours. For business inquiries via email, expect a reply within 24-48 hours during weekdays. Stream chat is monitored live during all broadcasts.
